# Code of Conduct

## Be civil

This project is a shared workspace. We expect participants to act in good faith and treat one another with respect.

### Expected behavior
- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent where reasonable; ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Disagree about ideas without attacking people.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.

### Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, threats, or intimidation.
- Hate speech or discriminatory insults.
- Sexualized content or unwanted sexual attention.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent.
- Persistent disruption, trolling, or bad-faith engagement.

### Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions; lock conversations; or ban participants at their discretion.

### Reporting
To report an incident, contact the maintainers privately at: **security@spitfirecowboy.com**.

### Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, and other official community channels).
